MACAON
MACAON is a tool suite for standard NLP tasksdeveloped for French. MACAON has been designed to process both human-produced textand highly ambiguous word-lattices producedby NLP tools. MACAON is made of several native modules for common tasks such as a tokenization, a part-of-speech tagging or syntactic parsing, all communicating with each otherthrough XML files . In addition, exchange protocols with external tools are easily definable.MACAON is a fast, modular and open tool, distributed under GNU Public License.
